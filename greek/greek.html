The Greek Mythology

Sardorbek Mukhudinov

Greek mythology is great way to see the way people thought and rationalized things back in the day. People always thought that there has to be something grander and bigger than them and seeing time of history when one specific (and boring) religion hasn't overtaken it yet. Boring, that is the best way to not describe what happens in Greek Mythology.

Zeus

The Greek pantheon's head honcho. The guy in charge of the sky and other gods. Father to many heroes and even gods, although most not from his wife. Youngest of his siblings.

Zeus

Hades

The Ruler of the Underworld where all the fallen reside. Feared by mortals just because he was handed short end of the stick when brothers were choosing their domains.

Hades

Poseidon

Ruler of Seas and the father of horses. Well respected in Greek mythology, especially by beta version of the Greeks, Mycenean Greeks, as he was the leader of the pantheon in their myths.
