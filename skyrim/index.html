Skyrim is an old, by the standars of our time, game from 2011 by Bethesda Softworks. Known by many as the game that defined the genre of open world games and set a new standard on how roleplaying games are made. While some did not seem to like the game's sudden impact on the gaming world, many upon many people have got a new passion for gaming as a whole because of it. Passion for gaming, coding, art and such. The game has had a working modding community keeping it alive and fresh even after so many years.
